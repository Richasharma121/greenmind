About Us

Welcome to our mental health awareness platform, a dedicated space created with a passion for promoting mental well-being and breaking the stigma around mental health issues. Founded by Richa Sharma, an artist, blogger, and content creator with a deep commitment to gender equality and women's empowerment, our platform aims to be a comprehensive resource for individuals seeking to understand and improve their mental health.

Currently in her third year of B.Sc. Life Sciences at Gargi College, Delhi University, Richa is also a senior member of the Fine Arts Society called Hues, where she has honed her skills in team building, time management, creative thinking, and communication. As a front-end web developer enthusiast, she has meticulously designed this platform with a green and beige color theme to create a calming and welcoming environment. Her dedication to making the website colorful and engaging, with pop-up effects for different sections, reflects her artistic vision and technical expertise.
Our mission is to spread awareness about mental health issues and empower individuals with knowledge and resources. We believe that mental health is just as important as physical health, and our goal is to make people aware of its impact on overall well-being. We provide information on various mental health disorders such as OCD, PTSD, ADHD, bipolar disorder, and depression, aiming to reduce stigma and promote early intervention.
The platform offers a variety of features, including mental health resources, blogs, user accounts, videos, podcasts, and downloadable materials. We also ensure accessibility with language options and other user-friendly features. By offering a directory of professionals, self-help tools, and crisis hotlines, we strive to be a valuable support system for our community.
Join us in our journey to promote mental well-being and create a supportive community where everyone feels empowered to seek help and support. Together, we can make a difference.
